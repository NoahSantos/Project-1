History of the W213 E63 AMG

The Present day E 63 AMG, the w213 is a beast of a car. With all its new technologies and super well constructed suspension and chassis. It has an all new high revving 4.0 litre dohc twin turbo v8 engine that puts out 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It also has a 9 speed automatic transmission that shifts insanely quickly. These engines are very tunable and simple bolt-ons they can make up to 900 horsepower.
